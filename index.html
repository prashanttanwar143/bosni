Our Heritage

Rooted in the
Royalty of Rajasthan

We are not a big brand with showrooms everywhere. But we are a brand built on a story, a purpose, and deep love for true craftsmanship. Our journey began with our family’s years in the jewellery business. One day, we saw our own Sutarla bangles being sold online at double the price. That moment stayed with us. It showed us the real value of our art and how the hands that create it often remain unseen and unappreciated. From this feeling,.

Inspired by the grandeur of Rajasthani palaces, the intricate meenakari of Mughal artisans, and the timeless kundan techniques passed through generations, each Bsoni creation is a tribute to India's most magnificent jewelry traditions.
